    FAQ

    • 1. What is the Student Management App?
      The Student Management App is a digital platform that helps schools, teachers, and students manage academic records, attendance, assignments, and communication in one place.
    • 2. Who can use the app?
      The app is designed for schools, teachers, students, and parents. Each group has its own secure login and access to specific features.
    • 3. How do I log in for the first time?
      You will receive login details from your school administrator. Use the provided username and password to sign in. You can reset your password anytime through the "Forgot Password" option.
